# Hi, I'm Mubasshera 👋

I build AI tools for people who are usually left out of the AI conversation — farmers who can't afford a soil test, Tamil speakers with no NLP tools in their language, communities living on top of slow-moving environmental disasters.

I'm a self-taught AI researcher from Tirupattur, Tamil Nadu. No lab, no supervisor, no funding — just a borrowed school computer lab after hours.

## What I'm working on

- 🌱 **Soil Nutrient Profiling (CNN)** — Computer vision model to detect soil nutrient deficiencies from field photos, built to run offline on a basic Android phone. 1st place, National Innovation & Technology Competition (600+ teams).
- 🗣️ **Tamil & Indic Language NLP** — Fine-tuning transformer models for Tamil and other Indic languages, which currently have minimal NLP support despite 250M+ speakers.
- 🛰️ **Satellite Image Classification** — K-Means based land-use and tree-cover analysis across multiple zones using multi-spectral satellite imagery.
- 🔥 **Coal Subsidence Early Warning** — Early-stage research using satellite thermal and interferometric data to detect underground coal fires and land subsidence in Jharkhand and Chhattisgarh before communities are displaced.

## How I work

Every project I build starts from a real constraint someone else is living with — no internet, no device, no language support — not from a clean dataset in a tutorial. I'd rather build something a person can actually use than something that scores well on a benchmark.
